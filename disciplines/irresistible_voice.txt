__**Irresistible Voice**__

**■ Amalgam:** Dominate 1

The Presence of the user becomes a conduit for Dominate. The vampire now only needs their voice to be heard in order to employ Dominate powers.

**■ Cost:** No additional cost

**■ System:** The users voice alone is now enough to Dominate a target. This does not apply to voices transmitted through electronic media such as phones, television, or apartment door-buzzer speakers.

**■ Duration:** Passive
