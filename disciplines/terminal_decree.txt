__**Terminal Decree**__

No longer hampered by the selfpreservation instincts of their victims, the vampire can now issue commands that directly lead to the harm or death of the victim. Mortals can be made to blow their brains out, jump from rooftops, or swallow poison. Vampires can, with a bit of effort, be made to walk into fire or sunlight.

**■ Cost:** No additional Hunger cost, but the Humanity cost is potentially severe

**■ System:** Terminal commands now must be resisted (see individual powers regarding rolls involved), rather than failing automatically.

**■ Duration:** Passive
